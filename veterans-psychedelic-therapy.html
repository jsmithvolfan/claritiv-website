A Turning Point in Mental Health Treatment

President Trump signed an executive order to accelerate access to psychedelic therapies for patients with serious mental illness, particularly our nation's veterans.

What the Executive Order Does

This landmark order directs the FDA and DEA to establish pathways for patients to access investigational psychedelic drugs, including ibogaine, psilocybin, and MDMA, that are currently under FDA review and have met basic safety requirements.

1

Fast-Track FDA Approvals

National Priority Vouchers for psychedelic drugs with Breakthrough Therapy designation, cutting red tape and accelerating review timelines.

2

Right to Try Access

Eligible patients can access investigational psychedelics (especially ibogaine) still under FDA review through expanded Right to Try pathways.

3

$50 Million in Federal Funding

ARPA-H matching grants for states investing in psychedelic research programs for serious mental illness populations.

4

DEA Rescheduling Initiative

Attorney General will review successful Phase 3 clinical trials for immediate rescheduling upon FDA approval, making treatments prescribable.
